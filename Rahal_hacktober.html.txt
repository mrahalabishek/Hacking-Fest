<h2> What is ransomware </h2>
<p> Ransomware is a type of malicious software that infects a computer and restricts users' access to it until a ransom is paid to unlock it.
 Ransomware variants have been observed for several years and often attempt to extort money from victims by displaying an on-screen alert </p>
